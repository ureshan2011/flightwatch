SMART FARE TIMING

Book your flight
at the perfect moment.

Faro watches the fares every day and tells you whether to grab the seat now or hold out for a better price — with the airline, stops and flight time for every option.
Faro refreshes several times a day (and on every update) — this page reloads itself to show the latest. Fares are gathered from Google Flights across rotating Chromium + Firefox browser profiles; airline logos from avs.io, live weather and currency from Open-Meteo and ExchangeRate-API. The buy / wait call is from a quantile gradient-boosting model trained on each route's own price history (heuristic fallback while history is thin). Informational only — confirm the live fare before booking. Open data is in data/.
