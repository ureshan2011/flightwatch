Book your flight
at the perfect moment.

Faro tracks fares daily and tells you whether to book now or wait for a better price — showing the airline, stops, flight time and booking links for every option.
Faro checks fares several times a day and refreshes this page automatically. Prices sourced from Google Flights; airline logos from avs.io, live weather from Open-Meteo and exchange rates from ExchangeRate-API. The buy/wait signals are generated by a machine-learning model trained on each route's own fare history. Informational only — always confirm the live fare before booking.
